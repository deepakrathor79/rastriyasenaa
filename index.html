About RastriySena

"RastriySena" is a youth organization working towards building a society based on justice, equality, and dignity.

Our primary goal is to follow the path shown by Baba Saheb Dr. B. R. Ambedkar, ensuring that every citizen gets their constitutional rights and human dignity.

We believe that an educated, organized, and aware society can bring true change. As Baba Saheb rightly said:

"Educate, Agitate, Organize."

These three principles form the foundation of our work.

Our organization spreads the messages of our great leaders and takes action—both legal and social—against those who insult them. We empower the youth by giving them a platform to stand up for social change.
